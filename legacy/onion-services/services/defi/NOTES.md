# DeFi Services

Onion services for cryptocurrency and decentralized finance privacy.

## Services

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `rpc_fortress/` | Personal Ethereum RPC tunnel via `.onion` |
| `zk_relayer/` | Privacy Pool / Railgun ZK-SNARK relayer |
| `atomic_swap/` | P2P cross-chain atomic swap coordinator |

## Key Benefits

- Eliminates IP-to-wallet linkability
- Prevents MEV front-running at the RPC layer
- Enables private DeFi operations from any location
