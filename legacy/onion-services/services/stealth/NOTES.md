# Stealth Services

Onion services for censorship avoidance and network stealth.

## Services

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `adaptive_pt/` | Auto-switching between obfs4/Snowflake |
| `ghost_rss/` | Anonymous RSS feed aggregator |
| `riseup_bridge/` | Onion-native mail proxy |

## Key Benefits

- Maintain connectivity in hostile networks
- Prevent tracking by news sites and CDNs
- Eliminate exit node sniffing risks
