# Communication Services

Onion services for private communication and sovereign storage.

## Services

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `vault_drop/` | Ephemeral file sharing with self-destructing URLs |
| `ghost_ssh/` | Secure SSH-over-Tor bridge |
| `ontology_sync/` | Distributed encrypted knowledge graph |

## Key Benefits

- No third-party servers touch your data
- Bypass NAT without opening ports
- End-to-end encryption by default
